About Akos Fragrance

At Akos Fragrance, we believe that a fragrance is more than just a scent; it's an expression of individuality and a reflection of one's personality. Our journey began with a passion for creating unique and captivating fragrances that resonate with people from all walks of life. Founded in 2020 by Stephanie Obeng Antwiwaa, Akos Fragrance has quickly become a trusted name in the world of perfumery. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has driven us to curate a diverse collection of fragrances that cater to various tastes and preferences. We source the finest ingredients from around the world, ensuring that each fragrance is crafted with care and precision. Our team of expert perfumers works tirelessly to blend these ingredients into harmonious compositions that evoke emotions and create lasting memories. At Akos Fragrance, we are dedicated to sustainability and ethical practices. We strive to minimize our environmental impact by using eco-friendly packaging and supporting fair trade initiatives. Thank you for choosing Akos Fragrance. We invite you to explore our collection and find the perfect scent that speaks to you.

Our Story

Founded with passion and elegance, Akos Fragrance was born from the desire to create unforgettable perfumes for men and women across the world. Each bottle is crafted with premium oils, luxurious notes and long-lasting wear.

From floral softness to bold oud warmth, our collection celebrates diversity, mood and personality. We want every spray to make you feel confident, powerful and unforgettable.
