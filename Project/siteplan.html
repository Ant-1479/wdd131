Site Plan

1.Site Name

Akos Fragrance

Akos Fragrance is a modern perfume brand offering signature scents for men and women. The name "Akos" represents simplicity, elegance, and a unique brand identity. It is easy to remember and aligns perfectly with a fragrance brand that values sophistication.

2.Colour Palette

The colour palette for Akos Fragrance is designed to evoke a sense of luxury and sophistication. It features a combination of deep, rich tones and soft, muted shades that reflect the brand's identity.

optional domain availability: akosfragrance.com

3.Site purpose

The purpose of this site is to present Akos Fragrance products, share scent details, tell the brand story, and allow customers to explore different fragrance collections. The site will include images, product descriptions, categories, and highlights to help shoppers choose the right scent.

4.Scenarios

  • Which Akos fragrance is recommended for women who love floral scents?
  • Where can I read the notes and description of each perfume?
  • What are the top-selling scents for men?
  • How can I find out about special offers or discounts on Akos fragrances?
  • Is there a way to contact customer support for fragrance recommendations?

5.Typography

The typography for Akos Fragrance is carefully selected to reflect the brand's identity and values. It features a combination of elegant serif and modern sans-serif fonts that convey sophistication and approachability.

Primary Font

Poppins is used for headings and important text elements, providing a clean and contemporary look.

Secondary Font

Montserrat is used for body text and descriptions, ensuring readability and a friendly tone.

Font Sizes

  • Headings: 24px - 32px
  • Body Text: 16px - 18px
  • Captions: 12px - 14px
