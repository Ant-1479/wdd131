Akos Fragrance – Website Plan

WDD 131 | Week 05 Project

Site Name

Akos Fragrance

The name "Akos Fragrance" was chosen to reflect elegance, identity, and originality. It represents a modern perfume brand offering luxury scents for both men and women.

Optional domain: akosfragrance.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Akos Fragrance website is to showcase and promote a line of luxury perfumes. The site will provide product information, brand story, fragrance categories, and a contact form for customer inquiries.

Scenarios

  • Which Akos Fragrance perfume is best for daily wear?
  • How can I contact Akos Fragrance to place an order or ask questions?

Color Schema

The color scheme reflects luxury, warmth, and elegance.

Primary Color: Deep Wine – used for headings, navigation, and footer

Secondary Color: Soft Cream – used for backgrounds

Accent Color: Gold – used for borders, highlights, and buttons

Typography

  • Playfair Display – used for headings and brand titles
  • Montserrat – used for body text and navigation
